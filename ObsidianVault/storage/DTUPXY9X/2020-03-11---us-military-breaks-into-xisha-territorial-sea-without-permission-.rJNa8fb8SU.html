US military breaks into Xisha territorial sea without permission

3/11/2020, 12:16:53 AM

Spokesperson for the Southern Theater spoke on the U.S. ship breaking into our territorial waters of Xisha

Colonel Li Huamin, a spokesman for the Southern Theater of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, said that on March 10, the USS "Mc Campbell" guided missile destroyer broke into China's territorial waters without permission. The Chinese People ’s Liberation Army ’s southern theater organized sea and air forces to track, monitor, verify, identify, and warn them to leave. Under the guise of "freedom of navigation," the United States has repeatedly shown muscles and provocations in the South China Sea. This is a hegemonic act that violates the rules of international law and is the source of the scourge that threatens peace and stability in the South China Sea. China has indisputable sovereignty over the islands in the South China Sea and its surrounding waters. The Chinese military is always on high alert and takes all necessary measures to resolutely safeguard national sovereignty and security and to maintain peace and stability in the South China Sea.
